Reconstructing...

Wenqi Huang 黄文麒

Pronounced: WEN-chee HWAHNG

Hi there! I'm a PhD student at the Chair of AI in Healthcare and Medicine, Technical University of Munich (TUM), supervised by Prof. Daniel Rueckert. My research focuses on novel representations for fast MRI reconstruction, with minimal or no training data.

Outside of research, my wife and I love exploring the world — hiking trails, birdwatching spots, hidden towns, and great Chinese restaurants. We collect fridge magnets and postcards from every place we visit. Occasionally I'm also a photographer or cyclist. We wish for world peace.
