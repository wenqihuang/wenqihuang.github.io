Wenqi Huang 黄文麒

Pronounced: WEN-chee HWAHNG

Hi there! I'm a PhD student at the Chair of AI in Healthcare and Medicine, Technical University of Munich (TUM). I was also a visiting researcher at Stanford University. My research focuses on magnetic resonance imaging reconstruction using implicit neural representations and deep learning.

On weekdays I study MRI, on weekends my wife and I love exploring interesting places around the world — hiking trails, hidden towns, or anywhere worth discovering. Occasionally I'm also a photographer or cyclist.
